About the archive

Hello, and welcome to the Wpwap government documents archive. Here you can find just about anything related to the Wpwapian government, including videos, photo, audio, and of course documents. The archive is structured as a series of pages, with a page for each topic and, if necessary, a subpage for a more specific topic. Everything in this archive has either been declassified and censored after being approved for public viewing or is in the public domain. This project was started on September 23, 2020, and as of the writing of this document, is still being updated today. If you have any questions or comments regarding the validity of the documents or anything in the archive, you can message me at frost#1633 on Discord, or visit my website at frostt.xyz for more contact information. However, I kindly ask to please not come towards me with unsolicited advice or ask for classified documents, as they will not be answered.
